<title>Build urban buildings</title> <h1><strong>Build urban buildings</strong></h1> <p>The dialogue is divided into four sections. <br>  top left is a selection list of available urban buildings<br>  bottom left is shown a picture of the chosen urban building<br>  top right some options may be set<br>  bottom right is information about the chosen urban building given<br></p> <h1>Selection list</h1> <p> In the selection list are given all urban buildings that are available with the options specified top right (<em>Residential houses</em>, <em>Shops and offices</em>, <em>Industrial buildings</em>). There are two tabs that will allow you to change how the urban buildings are identified.<br>  <em>Translation:</em> Shows the name of the building in the given language. If there is no translation available in the given language, the object name will be used.<br>  <em>Object:</em> Shows the Simutrans internal object name for the building type.<br> <br> The color of the text has the following meaning:<br>  <em>blue text</em> signifies a residential building.<br>  <em>green text</em> signifies a shop or office building.<br>  <em>black text</em> signifies an industrial building.<br> </p> <h1>Options</h1> <p>  <em>ignore climates:</em> This option deactivates the climate restrictions associated with various building types.<br>  <em>Use timeline year:</em> The selection list will only contain buildings that are available the current Simutrans year.<br>  <em>Show obsolete too:</em> Expand the selection list to also contain buildings that are obsolete the current Simutrans year.<br>  <em>Residential houses:</em> There will be residential houses in the selection list.<br>  <em>Shops and offices:</em> There will be shops and offices in the selection list.<br>  <em>Industrial buildings:</em> There will be industrial buildings in the selection list. <br>  <em>Rotation:</em> If the chosen building can be shown from different angles, then it is possible to chose a specific view of it here. The image bottom left will change to reflect this. If this option is set to <em>random</em> a random view of the building will be used.<br> </p>
